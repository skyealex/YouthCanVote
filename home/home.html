You(th) Can Vote!

Mission Statement

To inspire NYC Youth to become politically active, make an impact, and have their voices heard.

The youth is the future. Our goal is to increase participation amongst young voters in NYC!

Why Should You Vote?

Your vote is your voice and you deserve to be heard! Voting is much more than just electing your future president. Politics at every level have a huge influence on many aspects of your life. The politicians who you elect to office can impact College affordability, Health care, Action on Climate Change, Immigration policies, Gun Control and much more. Read more on our Why page to get more information on why you should vote.

How Can I Vote?

Voting can sometimes be a very confusing process and even inconvenient from time to time. However, that is why we are here to help! Click our How To Vote page to learn how to vote.
