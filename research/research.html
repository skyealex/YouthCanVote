Why is it important to research candidates before voting?

The politicians you elect to office in the US will represent you in our government!

They have a duty to make decisions in the interest of the people, but everybody has a different opinion of what will help our communities (from local, to state, to federal) thrive. With this in mind, you should think about what issues are most important to you and research your candidate’s stance on them.

For Example, If you are considering voting for Bernie Sanders and the issue of Energy and Oil is very important to you, searching simple things like “Bernie Sanders Energy and Oil” can offer clarity on his stance, and its alignment with your own opinions.
