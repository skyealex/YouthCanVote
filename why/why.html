Why Should You Vote?

"What do you care about? You future? Your family? Your community? What Issues do you care about? Poverty? Homelessness? The environment? Student loan debt?.... Now do you think about voting when you think about the issues you care about?"- Mindy Romero Ted Talks

Every vote counts! Voting is a fundmental right for every U.S. citizen. It gives you the oppertunity to have a voice and influence society. Have a say in what goes on in your community, state, and country. Express your beliefs and have an impact on what decisions are made.

According to "9 Reasons We Need Young Voters More Than Ever" by The Best Colleges "Young voters account for half of the voting population...yet older Americans are more likely to vote". It also states that "In 2016, only 19% of people aged 18-29 cast their ballot in the presidential election". The youth has the lowest voting turnout yet they are capable of having so much power in what goes in this in this country due to numbers!

This needs to change! Read more at https://www.thebestcolleges.org/9-reasons-we-need-young-voters-more-than-ever/
